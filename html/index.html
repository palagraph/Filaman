About FilaMan

FilaMan is a tool designed to simplify filament spool management. It allows you to identify and weigh filament spools, automatically sync data with the self-hosted Spoolman platform.

Get more information at https://www.filaman.app and https://github.com/ManuelW77/Filaman.

Spool Identification

Easily identify filament spools using NFC tags (NTag215 or larger).

Automatic Syncing

Seamlessly update spool data with Spoolman for accurate tracking.

OpenSpool Compatibility

Works with OpenSpool to recognize and activate spools on Bambu printers.
